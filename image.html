Portfolio

Project: Mobile App Redesign - Fitness Tracker The project involved redesigning a fitness tracking mobile app to create a more engaging and motivating user experience for health-conscious individuals. The aim was to enhance user retention and drive higher engagement with the app's features. Approach: Competitive Analysis: I conducted an in-depth analysis of competitor fitness apps to identify best practices and areas for improvement. User Persona Development: To better understand the target audience, I created detailed user personas representing different types of fitness enthusiasts. UX Design Iterations: With a focus on simplicity and ease of use, I iteratively designed wireframes and low-fidelity prototypes, gathering feedback from both users and stakeholders. Visual Overhaul: I reimagined the app's visual identity, introducing a fresh color palette and modern design elements to align with the app's core values. Gamification and Progress Tracking: To boost user engagement, I incorporated gamification elements and intuitive progress tracking to motivate users on their fitness journeys. Usability Testing: I conducted usability testing sessions with real users to validate the new design and gather valuable insights for further enhancements. Results: The redesigned fitness app saw a significant improvement in user engagement and retention rates. Users appreciated the new design's clarity and the motivational elements that kept them coming back to the app regularly. Positive reviews and word-of-mouth referrals contributed to a surge in new user sign-ups. Technologies Used: Sketch, InVision, Adobe Illustrator Key Achievements: 40% increase in daily active users within three months of the redesign. Average session time increased by 25%, indicating improved user satisfaction. Featured as a top fitness app on various app stores, leading to organic growth. This project allowed me to combine my passion for mobile app design and fitness to create an impactful product that positively impacted users' health and wellness journeys. Regenerat
